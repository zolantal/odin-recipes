Ragu

Traditional bolognese ragu.

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • White or red wine
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato puree
  • Milk
  • Salt & black pepper

Steps

  1. Dice the vegetables and saut√©e them.
  2. Add the beef and wine and stir until the wine evaporates.
  3. Add the tomato puree and cook over low heat for 4 hours, adding water if necessary.
  4. Add the milk, season to taste.
